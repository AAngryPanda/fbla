Welcome to Dreamland Park

Welcome to Dreamland Park! If you’re looking for a place where you could hang out with your friends and family for an affordable price, then you’re looking at the perfect place for that. We offer many fun rides to enjoy, a mini-golf course, and as well as have an indoor arcade with a lunch/snack bar. Our park also has scheduled shows where kids and parents can watch and enjoy. We also have birthday parties for parents who want to give their loved one something special to remember.
